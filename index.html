about

the story

Formed in Seoul, 2019, wave to earth is Daniel Kim, John Cha, and Shin Dong-kyu — three musicians who stumbled upon something rare: a shared sound that sounds like solitude, warmth, and rain all at once.

Their music draws from jazz, lo-fi, and soft rock, wrapped in a production style that feels intentionally imperfect — like a photo taken slightly out of focus, but somehow more beautiful because of it.

They don't chase trends. They make music for Sunday mornings, late nights, and every quiet in between.
